# Demo Script (2-4 minutes)

## Setup
- Open PersonaForge landing page
- Have dark mode enabled

## Narration

### 0:00 - 0:30 | Landing Page
"This is PersonaForge — an agentic AI platform that generates realistic customer personas, simulates interviews, and extracts product insights. Instead of spending weeks on manual user research, PersonaForge does it in minutes."

*Click "Try Demo"*

### 0:30 - 1:00 | Product Intake
"Here I can describe any product or startup idea. I'll use one of our demo presets — let's try the AI Study Assistant."

*Click the AI Study Assistant preset*

"The form auto-fills with a realistic product description, target audience, market, pricing, and stage. Let's generate."

*Click "Generate Personas & Insights"*

### 1:00 - 1:45 | Agent Pipeline
"Now watch the agent pipeline in action. We have 6 specialized agents running in sequence:

1. First, the Intake Normalizer processes the input
2. Then the Persona Generator creates 8 diverse customer profiles
3. The Interview Simulator runs realistic interviews with each persona
4. The Critic evaluates quality and diversity — if personas are too similar, it triggers a retry
5. The Insight Synthesizer clusters segments and extracts insights
6. Finally, the Launch Brief Formatter creates an actionable strategy"

*Wait for completion*

### 1:45 - 2:30 | Insights Dashboard
"The pipeline is complete. Here's the insights dashboard showing segments, pain points, desires, feature requests, pricing sensitivity, objection themes, and messaging hooks."

*Click through a few tabs*

"We can see 3 distinct customer segments with their shared pains and willingness to pay."

### 2:30 - 3:00 | Persona Explorer
"Let's look at a specific persona. Clicking into Sarah Chen — a 28-year-old grad student. We see her goals, frustrations, tools she uses, buying behavior, and objections."

*Click into a persona*

"Here's her interview — reactions to the product, desired features, trust concerns, and even transcript excerpts."

### 3:00 - 3:30 | Launch Brief
"Finally, the Launch Brief — a concise strategy document with target users, problem-solution fit, risky assumptions, messaging recommendations, GTM suggestions, and MVP feature priorities."

*Scroll through the brief*

"This is ready to share with a team or investors."

### 3:30 - 3:45 | Architecture
"For judges — the architecture page explains our 6-agent pipeline, the critique/refinement loop, and why this is genuinely agentic rather than one-shot prompting."

## Key Points to Emphasize
- Multi-agent pipeline with specialized agents
- Critique loop with retry logic
- Structured output with schema validation
- Real actionable insights, not just text
- NVIDIA NIM powering all inference
